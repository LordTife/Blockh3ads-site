- The Universe -

Funny & a whole lot of weird

We started as an exploration of what it means to be a "Blockhead." To us, it’s not about being dull—it’s about being sturdy. We create animated characters who are wonderfully stubborn about staying true to themselves and never giving up on their crew. They don't look like the real world, and they don't want to. Because in a world that’s constantly trying to "Block you up," we’re here to help you break out, explore a new POV, and remind you that life is too short to take yourself seriously..
